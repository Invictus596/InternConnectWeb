How does InternConnect work?

InternConnect connects students, colleges, and companies on a single platform to streamline the internship process. Students can search and apply for internships, colleges can track student progress, and companies can recruit top talent.

Who can use InternConnect?

Students, educational institutions, and corporations can all use InternConnect to find, offer, or manage internship opportunities.

Is there a fee to join?

Registration is free for students and colleges. Companies may have premium options for enhanced recruitment features.

How do I get started?

Simply sign up as a student, college, or company and follow the guided onboarding process to begin using InternConnect.
